COVID - 19 Simulation scenarios

These simulations are largely over-simplified of reality, please do not use these simulations as scientific proof. But i do highly recommend the reader of this to use these simulations for raising awareness against COVID-19.

In these simulations, every person will get infected on contact, the infection method does not matter.

Each "dot" in the simulation represents a person, when a person moves, it's movement is completely random, which does not accurately represent human movement.
